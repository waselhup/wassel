# A04 — Pricing always shows what-it-buys

**Date:** 2026-05-25
**Status:** Accepted
**Rule:** R04

## Context

Pre-Copilot Wassel showed users "200 tokens / month" with no clarification of what 200 tokens buys. Users couldn't decide whether to upgrade.

## Decision

Every token cost is displayed alongside its deliverable, in plain language. The pricing page (`/v2/pricing`) shows:

> 200 tokens give you roughly:
> – 1 Radar analysis
> – 1 ATS Resume build
> – 5 LinkedIn posts

The same translation applies in-product: when the user hovers a "Run Radar (149)" button, a tooltip explains "تحليل رادار كامل + مقارنة بالدور المستهدف" / "Full Radar analysis + comparison against your target role".

## Consequences

- The pricing table needs a "what it buys" column, populated for every plan
- Every spending button shows the deliverable as a tooltip / sub-label
- The post-purchase confirmation email lists what the wallet now affords

## Alternatives Rejected

- Show only "200 tokens" — rejected, users complained of opacity
- Drop tokens entirely, sell per-action — rejected, makes pricing variable and unpredictable
