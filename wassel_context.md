## 1. The Product

**Wassel (وصل)** is the Arabic-first AI platform that turns LinkedIn into a real opportunity channel for Saudi professionals.

Three core tools:

- **Radar** — Profile analysis with scored insights + improvement plan
- **Studio** — LinkedIn post generator (Arabic + English, multiple tones)
- **CV Builder** — ATS-compatible CVs using Harvard/MIT templates

**Live URL:** https://wasselhub.com

## 7. Channel-Specific Rules

### Snapchat

- Vertical 9:16 video, 6-second hook.
- Arabic colloquial, not Modern Standard.
- Bold text overlay, fast cuts.
- CTA: swipe up to `wasselhub.com`.

### LinkedIn

- Long-form carousels (10 slides) > short text.
- Arabic AND English versions of every post.
- Post Mon–Thu 07:00–09:00 Riyadh.
- Tag relevant Saudi companies (Aramco, STC, SABIC, etc.) when topically relevant — never spam.

### Instagram

- Carousel + Reel mix.
- Story-format for daily tips.
- Use Saudi-relevant hashtags only.

### TikTok

- 30–60 second educational shorts.
- Trending audio + Arabic text overlay.
- "3 mistakes on your LinkedIn" format works.

### WhatsApp (retention)

- Short, personal, no marketing tone.
- Template: `أهلاً [اسم]، لاحظنا أنك لم تكمل تحليل بروفايلك…`
- Always offer value, never push purchase.

### Twitter / X

- Short threads (5–10 tweets), Arabic.
- Mix of insight + 1 CTA tweet per thread.

### Blog (wasselhub.com/blog)

- 800–1500 word Arabic articles.
- SEO target: Saudi LinkedIn keywords.
- Each post → 5 LinkedIn carousels + 10 tweets + 3 Snap ads via `sayed.repurposeFromRss`.
